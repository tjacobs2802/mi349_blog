Hello! My name is Trevor Jacobs and I am the author of the Fantasy Football Blog. Currently I am a fifth year senior at Michigan State University, and made this blog as a class project. The goal I had in mind when making this was to be a host for anyone to give their opinions on the hottest fantasy football topics. Topics will be updated day to day, giving the users something new to talk about every day they use the blog site. This is not only a great way to get your fantasy opinions heard, but also a way connect with fans all across the globe. Thank you for using my blog, go Lions!

Fun football facts about Trevor:

  • Became a Lions fan when I was 9
  • First Lions game I watched was Lions vs Chiefs in 2011
  • First NFL video game I played was Madden NFL 11 on the Wii
  • My favorite NFL player is Calvin Johnson
  • An obscure player that nobody talks about that I love is former Seahawks RB Thomas Rawls
