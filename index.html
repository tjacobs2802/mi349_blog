Post: Davante Adams Situation

Author: fantasyfootballlover46, Published: 10/09/2024,

The Davante Adams situation is very intriguing. Everyone knows that Adams is leaving Las Vegas for greener pastures, there’s no doubts about that. However, where he lands could drastically change his value in both dynasty and redraft leagues. Right now the favorite is the Jets, to land him with his buddy Aaron Rodgers. From a glance, that looks like a pretty decent move. But taking a closer look, we can see that Rodgers already has some decent receivers in Allen Lazard and Garrett Wilson. Oh yeah, about Garrett Wilson, they did just fire Robert Saleh, so the chances his targets go up is going to increase a LOT in the next couple of games. However, one team that has been rumored is the Kansas City Chiefs. If he goes to the Chiefs, he will instantly be a top 5 receiver option in all leagues. Right now, nobody on the Chiefs roster (except for Travis Kelce, but he’s being doubled a lot this year) can even sniff the amount of production that Davante Adams puts up on a yearly basis. He would instantly become Patrick Mahomes’ favorite receiver, and it would allow Adams to get back to his prime Green Bay numbers.
